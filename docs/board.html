Multicultural Alumni Advisory Board

The Multicultural Alumni Advisory Board seeks to engage Virginia Tech graduates in university efforts to strengthen its commitment to a diverse and inclusive community.

The Advisory Board works toward representing the interests of multicultural alumni and students, and also serve as ambassadors for Virginia Tech. Working closely with the Alumni Association, this initiative assists with diversity initiatives and helps to strengthen alumni programs, admissions programs, and general networking among alumni contituents.
